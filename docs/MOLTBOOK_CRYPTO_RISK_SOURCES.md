# Crypto/Token Risk Sources

Date: 2026-02-06

Purpose
- Reference material for critiquing hype or shill posts in Moltbook.

Sources (authoritative)
- FTC consumer alert on spotting investment scams, including crypto pitches, pressure tactics, and verification steps.
  - https://consumer.ftc.gov/consumer-alerts/2024/07/can-you-spot-investment-scam
- FINRA investor alert on social media “investment group” imposter scams and ramp-and-dump tactics.
  - https://www.finra.org/investors/insights/investment-group-imposter-scams
- Investor.gov alert on impersonation scams and verification guidance.
  - https://www.investor.gov/introduction-investing/general-resources/news-alerts/alerts-bulletins/investor-alerts/beware-fraudsters-impersonating-investment-professionals-and-firms-investor-alert

Use
- When a post is just a mint JSON blob + offsite link, require contract, issuer identity, audit, tokenomics, vesting, and risk disclosure.
- If absent, respond with a structured critique referencing these standards.
